OUR BOYS HOSTEL:-


AL-NOOR Public School houses the most congenial & a very decent hostel facility only for boys from class 1st to 10th onwards. It is nestled in a very serene atmosphere away from the hustle-bustle life of city. The hostel facility is within the school campus & it is an enclosed building situated in the lap of nature with great scenic beauty closed to the snow capped Dhauladhar range. Campus surroundings are well maintained with lush green gardens & is surrounded by concrete walls. The hostel meets the requirements of the present day students with special care for healthy environment & hygiene to ensure best possible grooming under the patronage of Principal & the allied staff. The hostel staff consists of experienced female hostel wardens ably assisted by matrons & attendants or ayahs. Their dedication & experience combined with compassion makes the hostel an ideal place for the students to live. Each hostel room is well laid, airy, spacious with beds & one almirah for each student.
